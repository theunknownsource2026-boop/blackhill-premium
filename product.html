Premium Quality

The Classic Navy Suit is tailored from 100% Super 150s Italian wool sourced from the finest mills in Biella. The full canvas construction allows the jacket to drape naturally over time, molding to your body for a truly personalized fit.

The modern slim fit features a slightly narrower lapel, tapered trousers, and clean shoulders that create a sharp, contemporary silhouette without sacrificing comfort. Half-lined for breathability, it transitions effortlessly from cool autumn mornings to warm spring evenings.

Style it with a crisp white Oxford and polished loafers for the office, or dress it down with a fine-knit turtleneck for sophisticated weekend wear. A navy suit this refined is not just clothing -- it is a statement of intent.

Fabric Composition

  • 100% Italian Wool (Super 150s)
  • Full Canvas Construction
  • Natural Horn Buttons
  • Bemberg Cupro Lining

Care Instructions

  • Dry Clean Only
  • Store on a wide, contoured hanger
  • Steam to remove wrinkles -- avoid direct ironing
  • Rotate wear to allow fabric recovery between uses
  • Use a garment bag for travel and long-term storage
