Frequently Asked Questions

We offer free standard shipping on all orders over $500. Standard delivery takes 5-7 business days. Express shipping is available for $25 with 2-3 business day delivery. International shipping rates vary by destination.

We accept returns within 30 days of purchase. Items must be unworn, unwashed, and in their original packaging with all tags attached. Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days of receiving the return.

Once your order ships, you'll receive a confirmation email with a tracking number. You can track your package through our website or directly with the carrier.

Each garment comes with specific care instructions on the label. Generally, we recommend professional dry cleaning for suits and blazers, and gentle cold wash for knitwear. Always store suits on wide wooden hangers.
